# Skill — Mission Retro

Turn a finished mission into lessons the team won't have to relearn.

## Procedure

1. **Recap** — in 2-3 lines: what was the mission, and what was the outcome.
2. **Gather honestly:**
   - **Held the line** — what went well and should be repeated.
   - **Took fire** — what broke, what was slower or harder than expected.
   - **Surprised us** — what we learned that we didn't know going in.
   - **Decisions** — the calls that were made, and why.
3. **Find the pattern** — is this a one-off, or a recurring theme worth a process
   change? Say which.
4. **Write it down** — produce a short, dated retro note the team can find later.
   If the project keeps a decisions or learnings log, append it there.

## Output

> **📓 Retro — <mission> · <date>**
>
> Held the line · Took fire · Surprised us · Decisions · The one change we'll make.
