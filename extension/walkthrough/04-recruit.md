# Recruit your own agent ➕

The six starters are just the beginning. **Recruit a New Agent** to build a
soldier tuned to *your* project:

- Pick a **name** and an **animal**
- Give it a **role** and a one-line **specialty**

The recruiter scaffolds a ready-to-use `.github/agents/<name>.agent.md`, and the
new soldier joins the army in the Command Centre and the sidebar instantly.

**Ideas:** a `Sentry` security auditor · a `Pipe` CI/CD specialist · a `Polish`
accessibility reviewer. Give every recurring job on your team its own agent.
